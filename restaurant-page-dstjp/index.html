About Us
We serve awesome Street food in a relaxed environment no matter which of our restaurants you visit. We value hard work when we grind out meat every morning, we make our own dressages, taste test our burgets after our own liking but also due to our wonderful guests giving us tips about new flavors. A relaxed home-feeling where the guest and the personnel become one -THAT IS KRUBB. We want to be a meeting point for people that want to eat and drink good for low prices. Drop by for a lunch, order online, or bring your co-workers for an AW.
